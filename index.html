A little about Aastha

Visual craft aligned with performance and growth strategy.

I am a designer and growth marketer who sits at the intersection of brand strategy, visual craft, and marketing performance. I design identities that capture attention and build the campaign frameworks that keep it.

My visual style is rooted in editorial design principles—high typographic contrast, generous white space, and a deep appreciation for geometric order. I believe that digital products should be clear, highly readable, and cleanly laid out.

When I'm not designing interfaces, I'm designing physical product brand experiences, scripting content calendars, or creating growth marketing strategies that generate real returns.
